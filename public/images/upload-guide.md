# Photo Upload Guide

## Where to Place Photos

Place all photos in: `public/images/`

## Recommended Photo Names

### Hero/Homepage Photos:
- `hero-main.jpg` - Main hero image
- `hero-secondary.jpg` - Secondary hero image
- `team-photo.jpg` - Team photo (Fatjona and Labeat)
- `eyeglasses-hero.jpg` - Eyeglasses collection hero

### Service Photos:
- `service-eye-exam.jpg` - Eye exam service
- `service-contactology.jpg` - Contactology service
- `service-repair.jpg` - Repair service
- `service-consultation.jpg` - Consultation service

### Product Photos:
- `lenti-anti-luce-blu.jpg` - Blue light lenses
- `lenti-monofocali.jpg` - Monofocal lenses
- `lenti-progressive.jpg` - Progressive lenses
- `montature-collection.jpg` - Frames collection

### Gallery Photos:
- `gallery-1.jpg`, `gallery-2.jpg`, etc.

## After Adding Photos

1. Restart the dev server: `Ctrl+C` then `npm run dev`
2. Refresh your browser
3. Photos will appear automatically!

## Image Requirements

- Format: JPG, PNG, or WebP
- Recommended size: 1200px width or larger
- File size: Under 2MB each (for best performance)
